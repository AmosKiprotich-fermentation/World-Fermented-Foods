## Description
This fermented variant of irio represents spontaneous fermentation of mixed vegetables prior to cooking, practiced in some local contexts.

## Fermentation process
- Mixed vegetables crushed or mashed
- Short spontaneous fermentation
- Fermented mixture cooked before consumption

## Reported microbiology (examples)
- LAB: *Lactobacillus* spp.

## Functional relevance
- Flavor development
- Improved safety through acidification
