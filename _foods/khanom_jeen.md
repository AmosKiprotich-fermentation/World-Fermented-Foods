## Description
Khanom jeen are traditional Thai rice noodles produced from fermented rice batter, resulting in soft texture and mild acidity.

## Fermentation process
- Rice soaked and ground
- Batter fermented naturally
- Fermented batter extruded and cooked into noodles

## Reported microbiology (examples)
- LAB: *Lactobacillus* spp.

## Functional relevance
- Improved texture and digestibility
- Distinctive mild sour flavor
