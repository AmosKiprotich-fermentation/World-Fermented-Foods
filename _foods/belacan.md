## Description
Belacan is a traditional Southeast Asian fermented shrimp paste used as a flavoring agent, known for its intense aroma and umami taste.

## Fermentation process
- Shrimp mixed with salt
- Sun-dried and fermented
- Ground and shaped into blocks or paste

## Reported microbiology (examples)
- Halotolerant bacteria including *Tetragenococcus* spp.

## Functional relevance
- Protein hydrolysis and umami compound production
- Long shelf-life through salt fermentation
