## Description
Pla ra is a traditional Thai fermented fish product prepared with salt and rice bran, widely used as a condiment.

## Fermentation process
- Fish mixed with salt and rice bran
- Packed and fermented for months
- Liquid or paste used in cooking

## Reported microbiology (examples)
- LAB: *Lactobacillus* spp.
- Halotolerant bacteria: *Tetragenococcus* spp.

## Functional relevance
- Development of strong umami flavor
- Preservation of fish through fermentation and salting
