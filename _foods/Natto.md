## Description
Natto is a traditional Japanese fermented soybean product characterized by a sticky texture and strong aroma, commonly consumed as a breakfast food.

## Fermentation process
- Soybeans soaked and steamed
- Inoculated with a starter culture
- Fermented at warm temperatures (~40 °C) for 18–24 h
- Short maturation at lower temperature before consumption

## Reported microbiology (examples)
- Dominant bacterium: *Bacillus subtilis* var. *natto*

## Functional relevance
- Production of enzymes and bioactive compounds (e.g., nattokinase)
- Increased vitamin K2 content
- Enhanced protein digestibility
