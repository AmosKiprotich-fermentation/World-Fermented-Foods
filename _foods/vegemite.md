## Description
Vegemite is an Australian food spread produced from brewer’s yeast extract, a by-product of beer fermentation, and represents an industrial fermentation-derived product.

## Fermentation process
- Yeast biomass recovered from brewing
- Cell lysis releases soluble yeast extract
- Concentrated and blended into spread

## Reported microbiology (examples)
- Yeast: *Saccharomyces cerevisiae* (source organism)

## Functional relevance
- Rich source of B vitamins
- Iconic example of fermentation by-product utilization
