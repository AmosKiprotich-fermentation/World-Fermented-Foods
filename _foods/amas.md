## Description
Amas (also known as maas) is a traditional Southern African fermented milk with a thick consistency and sour taste, commonly consumed as a beverage or with staple foods.

## Fermentation process
- Fresh milk placed in a container and left at ambient temperature
- Spontaneous fermentation over 1â€“3 days
- Fermented milk consumed fresh

## Functional relevance
- Improved lactose digestibility
- Source of organic acids and live LAB
- Important in household nutrition
