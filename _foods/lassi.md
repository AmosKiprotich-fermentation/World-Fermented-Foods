## Description
Lassi is a fermented yogurt-based beverage consumed widely across South Asia, prepared in sweet or savory forms.

## Fermentation process
- Milk fermented into yogurt
- Yogurt diluted and churned with water
- Beverage consumed fresh

## Reported microbiology (examples)
- LAB: *Lactobacillus delbrueckii* subsp. *bulgaricus*, *Streptococcus thermophilus*

## Functional relevance
- Improved lactose digestibility
- Refreshing probiotic-associated beverage
