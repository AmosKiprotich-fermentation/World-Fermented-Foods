## Description
Nare is a traditional fermented condiment produced from African locust beans, widely used to season soups and stews.

## Fermentation process
- Locust beans boiled and dehulled
- Beans wrapped and fermented naturally
- Fermentation lasts several days

## Reported microbiology (examples)
- *Bacillus subtilis* group predominates

## Functional relevance
- Protein hydrolysis and flavor development
- Important source of umami compounds
