## Description
Lahpet is a traditional Burmese fermented tea leaf product consumed as a salad or condiment, notable for its sour, bitter, and umami flavors.

## Fermentation process
- Fresh tea leaves steamed
- Packed anaerobically and fermented
- Fermented leaves aged before consumption

## Reported microbiology (examples)
- LAB: *Lactobacillus* spp.

## Functional relevance
- Unique plant-based fermentation
- Cultural and ceremonial importance
