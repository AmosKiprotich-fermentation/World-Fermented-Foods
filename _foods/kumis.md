## Description
Kumis (airag) is a traditional Central Asian fermented mare’s milk beverage characterized by lactic–alcoholic fermentation and light effervescence.

## Fermentation process
- Fresh mare’s milk collected
- Inoculated with previous batch
- Fermented with frequent agitation for 1–3 days

## Reported microbiology (examples)
- LAB: *Lactobacillus* spp.
- Yeasts: *Saccharomyces* spp., *Kluyveromyces* spp.

## Functional relevance
- Improved digestibility of lactose
- Cultural significance among pastoral communities
