## Description
Attiéké is a traditional fermented cassava product from Côte d’Ivoire, prepared as steamed granules resembling couscous.

## Fermentation process
- Cassava grated and fermented
- Mash pressed, granulated, and sieved
- Granules steamed before consumption

## Reported microbiology (examples)
- LAB: *Lactobacillus* spp., *Pediococcus* spp.

## Functional relevance
- Detoxification and texture development
- Staple food with high cultural significance
