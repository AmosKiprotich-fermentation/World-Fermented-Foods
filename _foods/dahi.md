## Description
Dahi is a traditional South Asian fermented milk similar to yogurt, widely consumed as a staple food and beverage.

## Fermentation process
- Milk heated and cooled
- Inoculated with previous batch
- Fermented overnight at ambient temperature

## Reported microbiology (examples)
- LAB: *Lactococcus* spp., *Lactobacillus* spp.

## Functional relevance
- Improved lactose digestibility
- Central role in daily diets
