## Description
Placali is a fermented cassava paste from Côte d’Ivoire, typically consumed with sauces as a staple dish.

## Fermentation process
- Cassava roots peeled and soaked in water
- Spontaneous fermentation for several days
- Softened roots milled into a smooth paste
- Paste cooked prior to consumption

## Reported microbiology (examples)
- LAB: *Lactobacillus* spp., *Leuconostoc* spp.

## Functional relevance
- Detoxification of cassava
- Improved safety and digestibility
- Important component of local diets
