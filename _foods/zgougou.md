## Description
Zgougou is a traditional Tunisian fermented dessert prepared from Aleppo pine seeds and consumed during religious festivities.

## Fermentation process
- Seeds ground into a paste
- Paste mixed with water and fermented naturally
- Fermented mixture cooked into a pudding-like dessert

## Reported microbiology (examples)
- LAB: *Lactobacillus* spp.

## Functional relevance
- Development of characteristic flavor
- Cultural and ceremonial significance
